Project Overview

We have been hired as Data Scientists by the Center for Drug Abuse Prevention to help them target their marketing towards at risk groups of individuals. Based on data obtained from a recent study, we have used Machine Learning to create predictive models that can help the Center for Drug Abuse Prevention target where they should advertise. With this Tech, the Center for Drug Abuse and Prevention can effictively get their message to at risk parties and save lives.
